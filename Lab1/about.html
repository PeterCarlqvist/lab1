About Me

Biography

Peter Carlqvist, I have a formal education in chemistry, with a Masters in Chemical Engineering and a PhD in Physical chemistry. Since then I have studied health economcis, statisitics and programming. Currently I am working as a consultant in the Pharma industry, supporting companies with launching new advanced treatments in the Nordics. My skillset is well suited for demonstrating the value of new treatments as a broad set of different skills are needed.

Interests

Outside of work I enjoy spending time with my family, and I am a keen runner all year round! A passion is reading and I read all sorts of books, but mostly novels.

Future challenges

I hope to develop new tools to use within my business and to create a competitive advantage!
